About Us

E.CARS is a leading company in the United States that specializes in selling electric vehicles. With a strong commitment to sustainability and innovation, E.CARS offers a wide range of electric cars that cater to various customer needs and preferences. The company prides itself on providing high-quality, eco-friendly vehicles that are not only efficient but also stylish and technologically advanced. E.CARS is dedicated to promoting a greener future by promoting the adoption of electric vehicles as a cleaner alternative to traditional gasoline-powered cars. Their team of experts is passionate about electric mobility and strives to educate consumers about the benefits of driving electric. In addition to selling electric cars, E.CARS also provides excellent customer service, including assistance with choosing the right vehicle.

E.CARS offers free delivery of electric cars across the entire USA. Once an order is placed and details are finalized with the customer, our team arranges for the vehicle to be delivered directly to your home or office. We strive for prompt and reliable delivery so that you can start enjoying your new electric car as soon as possible.

E.CARS provides a warranty for all sold electric cars. Typically, the warranty includes coverage for a certain period of time or mileage, depending on the car model. The warranty usually extends to major components of the electric car such as batteries, motor, and other key systems. If you encounter any issues with your electric car within the warranty period, our team will be happy to assist you with repairs or replacement of faulty parts. E.CARS values the trust of its customers and aims to provide a high level of service, including convenient delivery and a reliable warranty for all sold electric cars.
